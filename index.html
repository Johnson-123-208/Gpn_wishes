Dear Friend,

The world all over celebrates the birth of Christ our Saviour.

Jesus our Saviour brings great joy, peace and harmony around the world. In this world of pain and sorrow He stands as a beacon of hope and healing to the millions who trust in Him.

The enemy knows his time is little and is doing his best to steal away many from the fold. But Christmas is a great reminder of the purpose why God sent His only begotten Son to save the world.

At Glocal Prayer Network we see God's Hand and faithfulness. His providence, care and redemption is what we experience every day. We see Him at work with our colleagues, vendors, clients and our friends and well wishers!

We take this time to wish you and your dear ones a Merry Christmas and God's blessings in the New Year!

Light and life to all He brings, Risen with healing in His wings:
Mild He lays His glory by, Born that man no more may die;
Born to raise the sons of earth; Born to give them second birth.

From Glocal Prayer Network Team
